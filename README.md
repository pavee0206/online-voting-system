A Full Stack Online Voting System built with Node.js, Express, and a real time results dashboard.  Features include poll creation, secure voting, live results tracking.

Project Description (Universal Framework)
Vortex Vote is a robust, full stack digital decision making engine designed to provide instant clarity in high stakes environments. The underlying architecture is a stream agnostic platform capable of serving diverse sectors:
🏛️ Political & Civic Stream
Purpose: Secure public opinion tracking and sentiment analysis.
Use Case: Large scale demographic polling for state-wide leadership contests (e.g., 2026 CM Race).
🎓 Academic & Institutional Stream
Purpose: Automated student council elections and faculty feedback loops.
Use Case: Eliminating paper based voting in universities through a mobile first interface.
💼 Corporate & Business Stream
Purpose: Real time consumer preference testing and internal board resolutions.
Use Case: Rapid market research for product launches or selecting "Employee of the Month" with live visual results.
🎭 Entertainment & Media Stream
Purpose: Interactive audience engagement and live "Fan Choice" awards.
Use Case: Reality TV show voting or social media trending polls where speed and real time bar updates are critical.
 
🚀 Technical Edge
Built with a Node.js/Express backbone, the system ensures data integrity across all streams, providing a "single source of truth" for any decision-making process.
